Comcast services such as X1, voice remote, and CDVR are some of the products we provide our SDC partners. RE-DC-ENT monitors, and supports the network 24/7 for our SDC partners.

SDC Remote hands engagement process.

Remote hands support at SDC location should be engaged by T&P Production support. Please send an email to them requesting to open a service now ticket.

Email : TP_Production_Support@cable.comcast.com

Phone : 1-800-896-5893

Step 1:

Comcast T&P production support identifies faulty equipment.

Comcast T&P production support opens a IOP ticket and through the bonding process, this will open a ticket on the SDC Remedy..

Ticket must include the following

The word RMA at the beginning of the title in the ticket.

Location of RMA. ( Site, Rack, and RU).

Date of Failure observed.

Serial number of RMA.

Date of Failure observed.

Serial number of RMA.

Name of RMA server and model.

Shipping address of RMA.

Contact person at Comcast for RMA with phone number and email.

Step 2:

SDC Video NOC receives RMA ticket via Remedy.

SDC Video NOC Specialist will dispatch ticket to tech and update the ticket with tech name, contact information and shipping address.

SDC open trouble troubleshooting calls if necessary between tech and Comcast.

Tech confirms the serial number of equipment fault and adds it to the ticket.

Step 3:

Comcast Data Center RE processes RMA.

RMA Equipment must be labeled NO COST WARRANTY REPLACEMENT to prevent Taxes.

Comcast must also provide the brokerage information for return.

Comcast update ticket with tracking information, Cost of equipment and return shipping address of RMA and the labels/number from courier/shipping Company.

Step 4:

SDC ’s tech receives replacement equipment at the headend and contacts Video NOC to work with Comcast on RMA swap.

SDC tech boxes RMA and Labels Equipment as FAULTY WARRANTY RETURN with the RMA number and Comcast brokerage information

SDC Tech update ticket with tracking number.

Step 5:

Comcast Data Center RE confirms faulty equipment is received and closes the ticket.
